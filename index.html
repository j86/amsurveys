The Practice

Atlantic Marine Surveys is an independent survey and technical practice operating across the Atlantic and Mediterranean.

The work covers condition surveys, pre-purchase inspections, damage assessments and on-site technical representation. If you need a clear, honest picture of a vessel — or someone to stand between you and a complex technical situation — this is the right place.

About

James Wright is a professional sailor and marine surveyor. The sea has been his working environment for nearly a decade — as deckhand, mate, skipper, and most recently as on-site technical representative for the refit of an expedition catamaran. He came to surveying the way most things worth knowing are learned: through the work itself.
